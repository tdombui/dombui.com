    Born in a suburb west of Philadelphia, raised in Southern California, and now residing in New York City — Dominick Bui is a multipurpose creator who focuses on photography, storytelling, designing graphics, and developing the Web. His photographic work incorporates several genres ranging from lifestyle, portraiture, landscape, abstract, and photojournalism; and has been featured by Monster Children, VICE, Complex, and a handful of other brands and independent publications.

    DB's style and approach to life are heavily influenced by skateboarding, Steinbeck, Moby Dick, techno, punk, shoegaze, rap music, Aphex Twin, Brutalism, computers, 90s Toyota, outdoor living, and the wilderness.

    dombui.com is for selected works. Follow the casual day-to-day excursions @doms_labyrinth.
